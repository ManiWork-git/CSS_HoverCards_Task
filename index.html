THE Hashiras

The Hashira are the elite warriors of the Demon Slayer Corps, each mastering a unique Breathing Style. The Water, Love, Stone, Sound, Mist, Serpent, Wind, Insect, and Flame Hashira protect humanity from powerful demons with skill and courage. Facing immense danger, they uphold honor and guide fellow slayers. Together, these masters of their Breathing Styles form the backbone of the Corps, inspiring resilience, strategy, and strength in the fight against darkness.
